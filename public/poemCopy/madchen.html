She is, above all, a drama of curves
caught in the tooth. A finger's trophy,

she maps the wills of our sires and dams
in fragments, the mistress of a circus

whose beasts roar and rut in silence -
shall daylight humble her, and the measure

of each of her arcs and scars strip apart
the last of our homeless, hidden humanity?

Her caverns host no mysteries any more,
and the stars in her heavens are gone.
